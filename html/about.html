Researcher Profile

Aejaz Ul Bashir

PhD Researcher · Electrochemistry Division · IIT Delhi

I am a dedicated PhD Researcher at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi, specializing in the intersection of materials chemistry and electroanalytical science. My research is focused on the atomic-scale engineering of MXene-based 2D materials to address the global challenge of sustainable energy production.

From early in my academic career, I have been fascinated by the complex electron transfer processes that occur at solid-liquid interfaces. My work today aims to harness these processes to enable efficient green fuel synthesis—specifically turning CO₂ into usable hydrocarbons and N₂ into ammonia, bypassing traditional energy-intensive industrial methods.

Beyond the lab, I am an advocate for open science and international collaboration. My experience as a Senior Research Fellow at the University of Szeged, Hungary, further solidified my belief that the next generation of energy solutions will be born from cross-border innovation and fundamental mechanistic breakthroughs.
